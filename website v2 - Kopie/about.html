Our Story

We are two brothers, Elyas and David, born and raised in Berlin. While studying, we dreamed of starting a company. Our passion for good food and our family’s roots in Spain motivated us to create a food company.

We import saffron from locally sourced companies that we personally know and keep in close contact with. This allows us to maintain strict quality control and ensure that every strand of saffron meets our high standards.

As new entrepreneurs, our top priority is to offer the best prices combined with exceptional quality so that our clients truly enjoy our products.

If you want to sell our saffron in your store or stores, use it in your restaurant, or simply enjoy it and cook with it, please do not hesitate to contact us.
