Our Story

We are two brothers, Elyas and David, born and raised in Berlin. While studying, we dreamed of starting a company. Our passion for good food and our family's roots in Spain motivated us to launch a food company inspired by renowned names such as Azafranes Carrera and Carrera Global.

We import saffron from locally sourced companies that we personally know and maintain close contact with. This allows us to uphold strict quality control and consistently deliver a product that meets our high standards.

As new entrepreneurs, our top priority is to offer the best price and quality, ensuring that our customers truly enjoy our products. If you want to sell our saffron in your store, use it in your restaurant, or simply enjoy it in your home kitchen, please don't hesitate to contact us.
